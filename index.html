My Story

Growing up I was always strong in math and I loved computers. My best friend and I would come up with the craziest stories to convince the computer lab teacher to allow us to use the lab during lunch. As a child, I aspired to become a 'Computer Scientist' that writes things for the computer.

I did not pursue Computer Science in college due to unforseen circumstances. I worked non-profit serving the community and used computer programs that were extremely inefficient. I realized that in order to make change, I needed to commit to my childhood dream.

Since embarking on this journey, hours and hours can pass in a blink of an eye when I am in the zone coding. I understand and appreciate the amount of problem solving skills, creativity and teamwork required to build something truly amazing.

Outside of programming, I am an avid Bay Area sports fan. I also enjoy reading and translating classic poetry from different cultures.
